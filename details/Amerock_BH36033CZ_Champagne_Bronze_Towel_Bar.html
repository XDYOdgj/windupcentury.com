Amerock Revitalize Champagne Bronze Towel Bar

Elevate the elegance of your bathroom with the Amerock Revitalize Champagne Bronze towel bar. Combining classic design and modern functionality, this towel holder adds a refined touch to your decor while offering practical benefits every day.

This sophisticated towel bar is part of the Revitalize Collection, known for its timeless style and quality craftsmanship. The Champagne Bronze finish brings a warm, luxurious feel to any bathroom setting, blending seamlessly with modern and traditional interiors alike.

Featuring durable acrylic construction, the towel bar is built to last and is easy to install with included mounting hardware, instructions, and templates. Its 18-inch height makes it perfect for holding both standard and oversized towels, ensuring convenience without compromising on style.
